Momos

Momos, originating from the Himalayan regions, are delightful dumplings filled with a savory mixture of minced meat or vegetables, spices, and herbs. Encased in a thin dough wrapper, these bite-sized treats are steamed, fried, or boiled to perfection. Served with a spicy dipping sauce, momos are a versatile and satisfying dish, perfect for sharing with loved ones.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water as needed
  • 250 grams minced meat or vegetables
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing all-purpose flour, salt, and water to form a smooth, elastic dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. For the filling, heat oil in a pan and saut√© the onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies until fragrant.
  3. Add the minced meat or vegetables and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook for a few minutes until the flavors meld together.
  5. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them out into thin circles.
  6. Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each circle and fold the edges to form a pleated pattern, sealing the momos.
  7. Steam the momos in a steamer for about 10-15 minutes until cooked through.
  8. Serve hot with a spicy dipping sauce and enjoy!
